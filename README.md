Tweaks and changes I thought would be nice to see in Vintage Story. All **fully optional** in the config, so you can turn off anything you don’t like. More features will be implemented as I come across new ideas!

**Current Features**:

* **Middle Click Sort**

  * **Middle click any item slot** in your own backpack or a placed inventory.

  * Items will be **stacked and sorted within the inventory**.

* **Quick Move Items**

  * **Shift + Left click on an item slot twice** in quick succession.

  * **All items of that kind will be moved** into the desired inventory.

* **Drag to Move Items**

  * **Hold Shift + Left click on any item slot**, then **drag your mouse over item slots**.

  * **All items hovered will be moved** into the desired inventory.

* **Leave One**

  * **Hold Shift + Left click while holding an item in your mouse**, then **drag it over any empty slots**.

  * A **single item will be left** in each empty slot, or **added to the stack**.

* **Quick Crates**

  * **Shift + Right Click on a crate twice** in quick succession.

    * **Moves all items that match the crate into the crate**.

  * **Control + Right Click on a crate twice** in quick succession.

    * **Removes all of the items from the crate**, stopping when the inventory is full.

* **Waypoint Sharing**

  * Use **/waypoint list** to find the waypoint ID.

  * **/wpshare `<player>` `<waypointid>`**

    * Shares a specific waypoint.

  * **/wpaccept `<player>` `<waypointid>`**

    * Accepts a specific share request.

  * **/wpaccept all**

    * Accepts all pending share requests.

  * **/wpdeny `<player>` `<waypointid>`**

    * Denies a specific share request.

  * **/wpdeny all**

    * Denies all pending share requests.

* **Clear Crafting Grid**

  * Idea credit: **Timbrage**

  * **Middle click on the output slot** of your crafting grid.

  * **All items within the crafting grid will be moved** to your inventory.

* **Clear Hands**

  * Idea credit: **Raathur**

  * **Shift + F** with an item in your hand.

  * The item will be **moved into your inventory**.

* **Squinting Zoom**

  * A **slight zoom** that is meant to imitate squinting, fully **customizable**.

**VERSION NOTE:** I always suggest the **latest version** of the mod for your game version unless specified.

**COMPATIBILITY NOTE:** If you have a mod that changes the number of backpack slots, please change the config's **BackpackSlots** to match.
